How am I finding it?

Sometimes I do think, "my god I have so much work to do". However, I wouldn't change my degree for anything. A degree in Computer Science can be what ever you want to make it. If you want to push yourself and venture into fields and topics which are harder then others, you can. Whereas, on the other hand, If you want to just learn the in class material and complete all of the set work, you can still walk away with a great understanding of your degree.

My favourite module so far is definitely Web Technologies. The reason why Web-Tech got my top pick is due to the flexibility of the final assignment. You can create what ever you want as a part of an assessed piece of work for the module.

Personally, I think I've learned so much from the final project. This is because I've been able to reserach loads of different ways to implement code regarding HTML, CSS, and JS; ofcourse at times it was frustraiting because a lot can go wrong, however I believe the best way to learn is by making mistakes and problem solving.
